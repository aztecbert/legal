Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bert's Supply ("we," "us," "our") handles personal information in connection with our internal SMS notification system (the "Service"). The Service is a private, internal accounting and inventory notification tool used by the registered operators of Bert's Supply. It is not offered to the general public.

Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to deliver operational text-message notifications to registered operators. Specifically:

  • Operator name
  • Operator mobile phone number
  • Date, time, and delivery status of each message we send
  • The content of messages generated by our internal accounting and inventory system

We do not collect information from the general public. We do not use tracking pixels, cookies, advertising identifiers, or web analytics tools in connection with the Service.

How we use the information

The information described above is used solely to:

  • Send operational text-message notifications to the registered operators of Bert's Supply (for example, accounting reconciliation results, inventory threshold alerts, and system status updates);
  • Maintain a delivery log for troubleshooting and audit purposes;
  • Honor opt-out requests (STOP) and respond to help requests (HELP).

No third-party sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, lease, or otherwise disclose any information collected through the Service to third parties. The only exception is our SMS delivery provider (Twilio Inc.), which transmits messages on our behalf and is contractually bound to handle the information only for that purpose. No information is shared for any other reason, including with affiliates, marketing partners, data brokers, or analytics providers.

No marketing use

We do not use the information collected through the Service for marketing purposes of any kind. No promotional, advertising, or solicitation messages are sent through the Service, and no recipient information is used to build marketing lists, audiences, or profiles.

Opting out

Any recipient can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from the Service. Replying HELP will return contact information. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Data retention

Message delivery logs are retained for as long as needed for operational troubleshooting and audit purposes, and are deleted when no longer needed. Operator contact information is retained while that person remains a registered operator and is removed promptly upon opt-out or operator removal.

Security

Operator contact information and message logs are stored on access-restricted infrastructure and are not exposed to the public internet. Access is limited to the registered operators of Bert's Supply.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or the Service may be sent to bertssupply@gmail.com.
